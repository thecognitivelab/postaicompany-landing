## Fact-Check Report — "11 People, $200M. Then 107 People, $500M."

### VERIFIED (8 claims)

- **Claim:** "Midjourney hit $200 million in annual recurring revenue" (2023)
  → Source: Sacra, Forbes (Mar 2026), Contrary Research. Multiple sources confirm. VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "Average publicly traded SaaS company generates about $300,000 per employee"
  → Source: Forbes (Paul Baier, Mar 2026), Redpoint Ventures. VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "Google does $1.8 million. Meta, $2.2 million."
  → Source: Forbes (Mar 2026), financial filings. Google 2025 revenue ~$350B / ~190K employees = ~$1.84M. Meta 2025 revenue ~$165B / ~74K employees = ~$2.23M. VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "$500 million with approximately 107 people — roughly $4.7 million per employee" (2025)
  → Source: GetLatka (107 employees at $500M ARR). Sacra reports similar. Math: $500M/107 = $4.67M. VERIFIED (with caveat — Latka is self-reported, flagged below).

- **Claim:** "Midjourney launched as a Discord bot in a public channel. Discord server hit 1 million users in 3 months and now exceeds 21 million members."
  → Source: Contrary Research, ProductGrowth. Discord server member count publicly visible. VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "Jack Dorsey cut 4,000 jobs from Block... citing 'intelligence tools' as the driver. Stock surged 24%."
  → Source: WSJ (Feb 2026). VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "Meta began laying off 8,000 employees — 10% of its workforce"
  → Source: CNBC (May 2026). VERIFIED.

- **Claim:** "Anthropic 2026 Agentic Coding Trends Report — 'The primary human role in building software is orchestrating AI agents.'"
  → Source: Direct from Anthropic report PDF. VERIFIED.

### APPROXIMATE (2 claims)

- **Claim:** "Midjourney: 11 employees and $200M ARR"
  → Range: 11-40 employees during the $200M ARR period. Forbes uses "11." ByteIota and Contrary Research cite ~40. The 11 likely refers to the founding/core team, while 40 was the operating headcount when $200M ARR was achieved. APPROXIMATE — both numbers are defensible depending on definition.
  → Recommendation: Lead with "11-40 people" or use "~11 at founding" with clarification. The Writer already flagged this in Editor's Notes.

- **Claim:** "Nearly 80,000 tech workers have been impacted by AI-cited layoffs in 2026 so far"
  → Range: Layoff tracking varies by source. TrueUp and Layoffs.fyi have slightly different counts. The order of magnitude (~80K) is correct. APPROXIMATE.
  → Recommendation: Add source attribution ("according to Layoffs.fyi").

### DISPUTED (1 claim)

- **Claim:** "$0 — Total venture capital raised since founding"
  → Dispute: Midjourney has taken $0 in traditional VC equity rounds, which the article correctly states. However, the $50M Meta partnership payment could be interpreted as external capital, even if non-dilutive. The article handles this correctly by calling it "non-dilutive — IP revenue, not equity." But readers may misinterpret "$0 raised" as "no external money ever."
  → Recommendation: The article already clarifies. No change needed, but flag for Editor awareness.

### UNVERIFIED (0 claims)
All factual claims have at least one source.
