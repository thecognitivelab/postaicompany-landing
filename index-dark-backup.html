Why this publication

Three things no other
AI publication does

01

We track revenue per employee as a north-star metric

Every other AI newsletter tracks product launches and funding rounds. We track the number that actually measures whether AI is changing how companies work. If revenue per employee doesn't move, the "AI transformation" is theater. Our data comes from SEC filings, company disclosures, and proprietary surveys, not vibes.

02

We build proprietary frameworks, not just commentary

The AI-First Org Chart. The Decision Velocity Framework. The Headcount Decoupling Playbook. These aren't blog posts, they're tools founders and executives use to redesign their companies. IP that compounds. Nobody else in the AI newsletter space is producing operational frameworks.

03

We prove the thesis with how we operate

Post AI Company is itself a post-AI company. Our podcast hosts are AI agents. Our newsletter pipeline runs on 12 autonomous cronjobs. Our research is AI-augmented. We don't just write about the post-AI company, we are one. Every piece of content is a live demonstration of the model we're documenting.
