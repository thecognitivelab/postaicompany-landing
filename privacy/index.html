Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Post AI Company ("we", "us", or "our") is a publication operated by SyntheticPerson.ai, based in São Paulo, Brazil. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data, in compliance with Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018) and applicable international standards.

1. What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate our publication:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to Post AI Weekly or submit a contact form
  • Name and message — when you use the contact form
  • Usage data — we do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or behavioral analytics tools. No third-party tracking is active on this site.

2. How we use your data

  • Email address (newsletter) — to send Post AI Weekly, our free weekly publication. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
  • Contact form data — to respond to your message. We do not add contact form submissions to our newsletter list without explicit consent.

3. Legal basis (LGPD)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent (Art. 7, I) — when you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a contact form
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 7, IX) — for basic site security and abuse prevention

4. Who we share data with

We use Resend to send transactional emails and manage our subscriber list. Resend processes email addresses on our behalf as a data processor. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third party.

5. Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months for correspondence purposes, then deleted. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your rights (LGPD)

Under the LGPD, you have the right to:

  • Confirm whether we hold your personal data
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of data processed with your consent
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)
  • Request portability of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fefaria@syntheticperson.ai. We will respond within 15 business days.

7. Cookies and tracking

This site does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. We use no third-party analytics scripts. The only external resources loaded are Google Fonts (typography) and the audio player for podcast content.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Our infrastructure is hosted on Vercel with appropriate security headers. API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables and never exposed in source code.

9. Children's data

Our publication is intended for business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via our newsletter. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints:

  • Email: fefaria@syntheticperson.ai
